About AnswerLM

AnswerLM was built for domain investors, marketers, and entrepreneurs who need more than gut instinct when evaluating domain names.

The domain aftermarket is a $2B+ industry — yet most investors still rely on rough comparables and subjective opinions to value names. We set out to change that.

Our scoring engine analyzes five core dimensions of domain value: brandability, market demand, SEO potential, monetization opportunity, and commercial intent.
